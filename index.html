Manifesto

Because every experts start as a noob.

Each person as the potential to become an expert. This is the purpose of NoobList : help people who want to learn about a discipline to get advices on the content they should use.

NoobList is an open educational resources plateform.

The principle is simple : a group of experts in a specific domain votes for best contents for learning about the discipline. The list of best contents is published on the plateform for free.

NoobList is a participative.

We donâ€™t believe that one person will have the absolute knowledge. Everybody can send a content on a discipline, this content will be reviewed by the group of experts for vote. If 80% of the group consider the content as pertinent, it will be add to the list.

We are in early-stage of the project, we currently collect discipline contents for expert reviewed.
Be appart of NoobList and help novices to become experts by sharing your best learning content.
