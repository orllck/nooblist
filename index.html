Manifesto

Because every expert starts as a noob.

Each person has the potential to become an expert. This is the purpose of NoobList : give people who want to learn about a discipline advice on contents they should start with.

NoobList is an participative educational resources platform.

The concept is simple : the NoobList community vote for best contents to know more about the discipline. The list of best contents is published on the plateform for everyone and for free.

NoobList is participative.

We believe on an infinite knowledge that only one person cannot have ! Everybody can send a content on a discipline. The content will be reviewed by the community for an approuval. If 80% of the community consider the content as relevant, it will be added to the NoobList of the discipline.

“Knowledge is Power, Power provides Information; Information leads to Education, Education breeds Wisdom, Wisdom is Liberation.
People are not liberated because of lack of knowledge.”
- Israelmore Ayivor
