NoobList.org

Start learning from the best contents.

Manifesto

Because every expert starts as a noob.

Each person has the potential to become an expert. This is the purpose of NoobList : help people who want to learn about a discipline to get advice on contents they should start with.

NoobList is an open educational resources platform.

The concept is simple : a group of experts in a specific domain vote for best contents to know more about the discipline. The list of best contents is published on the plateform for free.

NoobList is participative.

We believe on an infinite knowledge that only one person cannot have ! Everybody can send a content on a discipline. The content will be reviewed by a group of experts for an approuval. If 80% of the group consider the content as relevant, it will be added to the NoobList.

“Knowledge is Power, Power provides Information; Information leads to Education, Education breeds Wisdom, Wisdom is Liberation.
People are not liberated because of lack of knowledge.”
