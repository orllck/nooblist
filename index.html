Manifesto

Because every expert starts as a noob.

Each person has the potential to become an expert. This is the purpose of NoobList : to give advice on the content you should start learning with. Starting with quality contents will create your knowledge roots stronger.

NoobList is a platform of educational resources.

The concept is simple : the NoobList community votes for the best contents on a discipline. This list is published on the NoobList.org for everyone and for free. Because we know that sharing is the first step of learning.

We believe that knowledge is built together.

With NoobList, everybody can send a content on a discipline. The content will be reviewed by the community for an approuval. If 80% of the community consider the content as relevant, it will be added to the NoobList of the discipline.
